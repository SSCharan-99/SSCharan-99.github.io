Description:

Chatbot is a chat bot like Siri, Cortana and Google Assistant. It is located on our site as a chat box module. This chat bot helps the users to give the required information and can even navigate us to the particular page we want.
Therefore, this chatbot integrated with the website helps to the answer the queries raised by the students those who want to join in MSIT course.
